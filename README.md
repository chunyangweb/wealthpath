# Wealthpath

> Project your personal cash flow and check liquidity across French savings and investment products.

A static web app that helps you simulate your future cash position based on how you allocate across **Livret A, LDDS, LEP, CEL, PEL, PEA, assurance-vie, PER, CTO** and a user-defined custom product. Two distinct views: a **projection** of total cash position, and a **liquidity** view that tells you when your money is actually accessible.

**Live:** https://chunyangweb.github.io/wealthpath

**100% client-side.** No accounts, no server, no tracking. Your data stays in your browser's localStorage.
